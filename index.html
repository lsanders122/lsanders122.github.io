Chihuahuas

This website will mainly be about the greatest dogs to exist: Chihuahuas. These dogs are relatively small, between the height of 6-9 inches and weigh about 3 and 6 pounds. The typical lifespan of a chihuahua is between 12-20 years, depending on how well the owner cares for their chihuahua. Chihuahuas are very noisy and loud, desptie their small frames, there are different types of chihuahuas, such as a shortcoat and longcoat.

Applehead Chihuahuas

These dogs are known for their large heads, which is where the word "apple head" comes from
